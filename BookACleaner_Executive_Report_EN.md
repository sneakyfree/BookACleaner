## 6. Monetization Strategy

### Philosophy: "Free to Grow, Premium to Scale"

**Core platform is FREE** — Essential for marketplace cold-start.

### Revenue Streams (Priority Order)

| Stream | How It Works | Projected Revenue (Mo 12) |
|--------|--------------|---------------------------|
| **Display Ads** | Google AdSense banners | $8,000/mo |
| **Sponsored Profiles** | Cleaners pay for visibility | $5,000/mo |
| **Freemium Upgrades** | Pro tier at $29/mo | $5,000/mo |
| **Transaction Fees** | 5-10% (future) | $3,000/mo |
| **Total** | | **$21,000/mo** |

### Payment Flow

1. **Hirer pays upfront** via Stripe
2. **Funds held in escrow** until job complete
3. **24-hour review window** for disputes
4. **Auto-release** to cleaner (minus future platform fee)

### Two-Sided Reviews
- Both parties rate each other
- Cleaners can see hirer ratings before accepting
- Quality signals (not just price) in marketplace
